Hey 👋 I'm Chris

Chris, a 24-year-old Canadian-born Filipino designer from Calgary, Alberta, brings a chill vibe to his work. Armed with a Marketing degree from the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, he's all about infusing creativity and innovation into everything he does.

With a diverse background spanning the public sector with the City of Calgary and the dynamic startup scene with Neo Financial, Chris thrives on challenges. At Neo, he wore many hats, from forming cool partnerships to leading creative design projects and jazzing up product enhancements.

Outside of the boardroom, You can find Chris at the Studio. Chris is part of the Calgary Surge basketball dance team. Outside of the studio, you can find him training for next marathon. Living life with enthusiasm, Chris loves pushing for excellence and exploring natures wonders.

I hold a degree in Marketing, but I'm currently pursuing further education in user experience design. My aim is to leverage my marketing background to craft compelling digital experiences.

I have quite a few, besides making custom rugs and dancing - I really enjoy going out into the mountains and exploring nature. I also really like keeping up with anything tech related. Before I worked in Corporate I actually worked at Apple before pursuing my career in marketing.
